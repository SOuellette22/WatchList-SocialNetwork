# WatchList-SocialNetwork

A social watchlist platform where friends share, rate, and react to the shows and movies they're watching.

## About

WatchTogether is a social network built around what you watch. Create and manage your personal watchlist, track what you've seen, and stay connected with friends through the shows and movies you love.

### How It Works

- **Track Your Watching** — Maintain a *watch later* list and a *watched* list for shows and movies. Rate anything you've watched to help your friends decide what to watch next.
- **Connect With Friends** — Add friends and browse their watchlists. See what they've watched, what they're planning to watch, and how they rated it.
- **React and Engage** — Spot a show on a friend's watch-later list that you've already seen? Drop an emoji reaction they'll see on their end to let them know what they're in for.
- **Stay in the Loop** — When a friend rates a show or movie that's on your watch-later list, you'll get a notification with their rating so you can decide whether to bump it up or skip it.
- **Recommend With Purpose** — Send show and movie suggestions directly to friends. They can preview your recommendation and add it to their watch-later list with one tap.
- **Friend-Only Ratings** — When you tap into a show or movie, you'll see ratings from your friends — not strangers. Recommendations you trust from people you know.
